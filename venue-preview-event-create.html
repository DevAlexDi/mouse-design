The Gold Circle Club

@goldcircleclub

About

Since its stellar opening in 2003, The Gold Circle Club is a high-profile Euro-style night club that hosted many notables and celebrities over the years. It's a colorful, multi-tiered dance club featuring 30-foot ceilings, LED screens & plenty of night owls. Big-name DJs and a dynamic sound system keep the dance floor pumping at this establishment.
